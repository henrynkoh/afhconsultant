# AFH Consultant Curriculum - Step-by-Step Tutorial

## Welcome to Your Learning Journey!

This tutorial will guide you through the complete AFH Success Consultant Curriculum, from understanding the basics to mastering advanced concepts.

**Structure**: 7 modules + practice exam + hands-on tools
**Goal**: Become a certified AFH Success Consultant
