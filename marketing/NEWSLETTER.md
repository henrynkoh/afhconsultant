## Newsletter Best Practices

### Subject Line Tips
- Keep under 50 characters
- Use numbers and emojis sparingly
- Create urgency or curiosity
- Personalize when possible

### Content Tips
- One main message per email
- Clear call-to-action
- Mobile-friendly design
- Value-focused content

### Sending Frequency
- Weekly: Tips and updates
- Monthly: Summary and highlights
- As needed: Important announcements

### Segmentation
- New subscribers: Welcome series
- Students: Educational content
- Consultants: Advanced strategies
- Owners: Direct application tips
