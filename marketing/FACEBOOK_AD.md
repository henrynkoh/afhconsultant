## Budget Recommendations

- **Daily Budget**: $50-$200
- **Campaign Duration**: 30-90 days
- **Bid Strategy**: Lowest Cost or Cost Cap
- **Optimization**: Conversions or Landing Page Views

---

## A/B Testing Ideas

1. Headline variations (Problem vs. Solution)
2. Image styles (Professional vs. Casual)
3. CTA buttons (Learn More vs. Get Started)
4. Ad formats (Single Image vs. Carousel)
5. Text length (Short vs. Long)

---

## Tracking & Metrics

**Key Metrics to Monitor:**
- Click-Through Rate (CTR): Target 2%+
- Cost Per Click (CPC): Target <$2
- Conversion Rate: Target 5%+
- Cost Per Acquisition (CPA): Target <$100
- Return on Ad Spend (ROAS): Target 3:1+

## Compliance Notes

- Ensure all claims are truthful and verifiable
- Include disclaimers for educational content
- Follow Facebook's healthcare advertising policies
- Respect privacy regulations (HIPAA considerations)
