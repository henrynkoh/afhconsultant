## Best Practices for Threads

1. **Be Conversational**: Threads is text-focused, so write naturally
2. **Use Threads**: Break long content into multiple posts
3. **Engage Actively**: Respond to comments quickly
4. **Share Value**: Focus on education, not just promotion
5. **Be Authentic**: Show personality and expertise
6. **Use Hashtags**: But don't overdo it (3-5 per post)
7. **Link Strategically**: Use "Link in bio" for CTAs
