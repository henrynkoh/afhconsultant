## Best Practices

1. **Visual Consistency**: Use brand colors and fonts
2. **Authentic Content**: Show real results, not stock photos
3. **Engage Actively**: Respond to comments and DMs
4. **Use Stories**: Daily stories increase visibility
5. **Hashtag Strategy**: Mix popular and niche hashtags
6. **Post Timing**: Test optimal posting times
7. **Video Content**: Reels get more reach than static posts
