## Email Best Practices

### Subject Lines
- Keep under 50 characters
- Use personalization: [First Name]
- Create urgency or curiosity
- Test different approaches

### Content
- One main message per email
- Clear call-to-action
- Mobile-friendly design
- Value-focused content

### Timing
- Welcome series: Immediate
- Weekly tips: Same day/time each week
- Promotional: Based on campaign goals
- Re-engagement: 30/60/90 days inactive

### Segmentation
- New subscribers
- Active students
- Inactive users
- Graduates/Consultants
- Geographic (Washington State)

### A/B Testing
- Subject lines
- Send times
- CTA buttons
- Content length
- Personalization

## Metrics to Track

- Open Rate: Target 25%+
- Click-Through Rate: Target 3%+
- Conversion Rate: Target 1%+
- Unsubscribe Rate: Keep under 0.5%
- Bounce Rate: Keep under 2%
