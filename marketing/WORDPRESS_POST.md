<h2>The Problem: The Medicaid Trap</h2>
<p>Most AFH owners start with the best intentions: they want to provide quality care to seniors. But they fall into what we call "the Medicaid trap":</p>

<ul>
<li>Accepting low-paying Medicaid residents ($4,000-$4,500/month)</li>
<li>Working as caregivers instead of business administrators</li>
<li>Using flat-rate pricing instead of tiered models</li>
<li>Missing opportunities for private-pay residents ($8,500-$12,000/month)</li>
</ul>

<h3>The Result?</h3>
<ul>
<li>Exhausted owners working 24/7</li>
<li>Thin profit margins ($500-$1,000/month per resident)</li>
<li>High staff turnover</li>
<li>Burnout and business failure</li>
</ul>

<p>After working with hundreds of AFH owners, we've identified three critical pillars that separate successful businesses from struggling ones:</p>

<h3>Pillar 1: Financial Engineering & Viability</h3>
<h4>Tiered Pricing Models</h4>
<p>Instead of flat-rate pricing, successful AFH owners implement tiered pricing based on level of care:</p>

</tr>

<h4>Add-On Services</h4>
<p>Smart owners bill separately for:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Nurse Delegation:</strong> $250-$500/month</li>
<li><strong>Incontinence Management:</strong> $300-$600/month</li>
<li><strong>Two-Person Transfers:</strong> $1,500/month</li>
<li><strong>Specialty Diets:</strong> $200-$400/month</li>
</ul>

<h4>The Daily Rate Advantage</h4>
<p>Using daily rates instead of monthly rates ensures you get paid for the 31st day:</p>
<ul>
<li>Monthly Rate: $8,500</li>
<li>Daily Rate: ($8,500 × 12) ÷ 365 = $279.45/day</li>
<li>Extra Revenue: $8,500/year per resident</li>
</ul>

<h3>Pillar 2: Medication System Integrity (WAC 388-76-10430)</h3>
<p>Compliance isn't just about avoiding citations—it's about protecting your license and your residents.</p>

<h4>Common Violations:</h4>
<ul>
<li>Ditto marks on Medication Administration Records (MAR)</li>
<li>Missing signature keys</li>
<li>Improper narcotic storage</li>
<li>Incomplete documentation</li>
</ul>

<h4>The Cost of Non-Compliance:</h4>
<ul>
<li>State citations: $500-$5,000 per violation</li>
<li>License suspension: Potential business closure</li>
<li>Legal liability: Lawsuits and settlements</li>
</ul>

<h3>Pillar 3: Resident Rights & Disclosures (WAC 388-10510+)</h3>
<p>Complete disclosure packets protect both residents and owners.</p>

<h4>Required Documents:</h4>
<ul>
<li>Notice of Rights and Services</li>
<li>Disclosure of Services (DSHS 10-508)</li>
<li>Disclosure of Charges (DSHS 15-449)</li>
<li>Medicaid Conversion Policy (14-point font)</li>
</ul>
